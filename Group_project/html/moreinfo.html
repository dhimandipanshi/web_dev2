ABOUT US


Fashion X spread happiness by turning an everyday essential into a colorful design piece with a rigid standard of ultimate quality, craftsmanship and creativity. a concept now brought to perfection by the fashion x collective of creators. today fashion x are sold in more than 90 countries and on every continent. fashion x features an almost endless variety of models and designs, using a broad spectrum of color combinations and original patterns simply put: there's a pair of high-quality garments and accesories for every occasion, mindset and style.

Reformation began by selling vintage clothing out of a small Los Angeles storefront in 2009. We quickly expanded into making our own stuff, with a focus on sustainability. Today, we make effortless silhouettes that celebrate the feminine figure and pioneer sustainable practices, focusing on people and progress each step of the way.
